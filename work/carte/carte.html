Carte üéØ - Intergrated Product Design of Digital Building Blocks

'Carte' is an integrated product design project that aims to help children develop spatial thinking skills in a fun way.

The project focuses on the broad application of STEM education foundations in value creation and everyday life, recognizing that children have differences in spatial perception that may affect future learning. Therefore, the project developed an organized product that enables young children to better cultivate spatial thinking during play.
