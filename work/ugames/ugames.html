UGames üïπÔ∏è - A BoardGame Social Network

UGames is an innovative digital platform designed to connect board game enthusiasts of all experience levels, fostering a vibrant community centered around the shared passion for tabletop gaming. At its core, the service provides a comprehensive suite of tools for organizing, discovering, and participating in board game events, catering to a diverse user base ranging from curious newcomers to seasoned veterans and established gaming associations.



User Studies

UGames aims to differentiate itself by offering a more comprehensive, tailored experience that seamlessly integrates event management, social networking, and game tracking in a single, user-friendly platform. By focusing specifically on the needs of the board gaming community and providing tools that cater to players of all levels, from newcomers to veteran organizers, this service seeks to create a more cohesive and engaging ecosystem for board game enthusiasts.

The platform's main functionalities include a robust event management system, allowing users to create public and private gaming sessions with detailed information such as game type, player count, location, and time. A user-friendly calendar interface displays all upcoming events, making it easy for members to plan their gaming schedule. The service also incorporates a friend system with tagging capabilities, enabling users to connect with like-minded players and organize their contacts based on gaming preferences or availability.
