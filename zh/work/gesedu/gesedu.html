Ges.edu👌🏻- Interactive Education Experience

(English placeholder) Ges.edu is a gesture-assisted early childhood education platform that innovatively integrates gesture-based interaction into learning experiences to enhance communication and adaptability. It’s interesting to find that gestures use could help a lot with our daily expression and even the learning process. Besides, with new technology, gestures were developed for more scenarios. Then, what will be there if gestures could be used into children’s education？ There will be the opportunity for both their learning progress and future adaptability to such interaction modes.
